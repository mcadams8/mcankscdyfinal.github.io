SERVICES

The Prep

What's better than takeout? Your very own gourmet chef! When you book one of our chefs for your event, you won't have to worry about grocery shopping and prep work. Your chef will come prepared with the freshest of ingredients to produce flavorful dishes for you to enjoy.

The Main Event

During the event, your chef will be your host and take care of all of the serving. All you have to do is sit back, relax, and enjoy the exquisite culinary creations our chefs serve you in the comfort of your own home.

The Clean Up

When the event wraps up, there's no need to worry about dirty dishes or scrubbing pots and pans. Our chefs will make sure to wash the dishes and leave your kitchen spotless.

PRICING

The cost of booking one of our private chefs varies according the number of guests being served, ingredients necessary for your desired menu, transportation, cleaning, and chef's labor.
